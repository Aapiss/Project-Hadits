About This Web

A hadith is a report concerning the sayings, actions, or approvals of Prophet Muhammad, which serves as a source of teachings and guidance in Islam, alongside the Quran. Hadiths are classified as sahih (authentic), hasan (good), da'if (weak), or mawdu' (fabricated). Sahih hadiths are the most reliable and are used to interpret the Quran and provide guidance in religious practices. Hadiths are a key element in understanding and practicing the religion of Islam.
